About

Bold or Italic is an atelier founded by designer and artist Wei Li. Bold or Italic produces creative works across a wide range of media, including product design, art installations, set design, short films and photography. Bold or Italic combinines provocative ideas and stimulating imagery to evoke eyegasms and mental warp.

As a graduate of Stanford’s M.F.A. in Product Design program, Li uses her work to explore fantasies and desires with visual, visceral and whimsical language. Incorporating pure sensations to push the boundaries of people's comfort zones, Li “pokes” things in a provocative manner that some refer to as “intriguingly uncomfortable.”She hopes to evoke a direct emotional response from her audience and for them to become empowered enough to submit to their own fantasies. Wei currently lives and works in San Francisco.
